# E-Commerce Customer Support FAQs

## Order Management

### How can I track my order?
You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can also use the tracking number on our shipping partner's website.

### How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. International orders may take 10-15 business days depending on the destination.

### Can I modify my order after placing it?
Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement. After that, please contact customer support immediately. Once an order is being processed, modifications may not be possible.

### What if my order hasn't arrived?
If your order hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe, please check the tracking information first. If there's no update or the package is delayed, contact us with your order number and we'll investigate.

## Returns & Refunds

### What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with tags attached. Some items like electronics and personalized products may have different return policies.

### How do I initiate a return?
You can initiate a return by logging into your account, going to "My Orders", selecting the order, and clicking "Return Item". You'll receive a return authorization and shipping label.

### How long do refunds take?
Once we receive and process your returned item, refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

### Can I return items without a receipt?
Returns without a receipt may be accepted for store credit only, subject to verification. We recommend keeping your order confirmation email.

### What items cannot be returned?
Personalized items, intimate apparel, perishable goods, and items marked as "final sale" cannot be returned. Electronics must be returned within 14 days and in original packaging.

## Payment & Billing

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for certain orders.

### Why was my payment declined?
Payment declines can occur due to insufficient funds, incorrect card information, bank security measures, or expired cards. Please verify your payment information and contact your bank if issues persist.

### Can I change my payment method after ordering?
Payment method changes are not possible after an order is placed. If payment fails, you'll need to place a new order with the correct payment method.

### Do you offer payment plans?
Yes, we offer installment payment plans through our financing partners for orders over $500. You can select this option at checkout.

### When will I be charged?
You'll be charged when your order is confirmed. For pre-orders, you may be charged when the item ships.

## Shipping & Delivery

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Some items may have shipping restrictions.

### Can I change my shipping address?
You can change your shipping address within 1 hour of placing your order. After that, contact customer support immediately. Once shipped, address changes may not be possible.

### What if my package is damaged?
If your package arrives damaged, please take photos and contact us within 48 hours. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund.

### Do you offer same-day delivery?
Same-day delivery is available in select metropolitan areas for orders placed before 2 PM. Additional fees apply.

## Product Information

### How do I know if an item is in stock?
Items show "In Stock" or "Out of Stock" status on the product page. You can also sign up for back-in-stock notifications.

### Can I cancel a pre-order?
Pre-orders can be cancelled before the item ships. Once shipped, standard return policies apply.

### Do you offer product warranties?
Most electronics come with manufacturer warranties. Extended warranties are available for purchase at checkout for eligible items.

## Account & Security

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive a password reset link.

### How do I update my account information?
Log into your account and go to "Account Settings" to update your information, including email, address, and payment methods.

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and never store your full payment details. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.
