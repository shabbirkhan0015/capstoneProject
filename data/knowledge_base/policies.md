# E-Commerce Store Policies

## Return Policy

### General Returns
- **Return Window**: 30 days from delivery date
- **Condition**: Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached
- **Return Method**: Use the return portal in your account or contact customer support
- **Return Shipping**: Free returns for items over $50; otherwise, return shipping costs apply

### Exceptions
- Electronics: 14-day return window, must include all original accessories and packaging
- Personalized items: No returns accepted
- Intimate apparel: No returns for hygiene reasons
- Final sale items: Clearly marked and non-returnable
- Perishable goods: No returns accepted

### Refund Processing
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item
- Refunds issued to original payment method
- Store credit available for returns without receipt (subject to verification)

## Shipping Policy

### Domestic Shipping
- **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days, $5.99 flat rate (free over $50)
- **Express Shipping**: 2-3 business days, $12.99
- **Overnight Shipping**: Next business day, $24.99 (orders before 2 PM)

### International Shipping
- Shipping times: 10-15 business days (varies by country)
- Shipping costs calculated at checkout
- Customer responsible for customs duties and taxes
- Some items restricted by destination country

### Order Processing
- Orders typically process within 1-2 business days
- Processing may take longer during peak seasons or for custom items
- You'll receive email confirmation with tracking information once shipped

## Privacy Policy

### Data Collection
We collect information necessary to process orders, including:
- Name, email, shipping address, billing address
- Payment information (processed securely, not stored)
- Order history and preferences
- Website usage data for analytics

### Data Usage
- Order processing and fulfillment
- Customer service and support
- Marketing communications (with consent)
- Website improvement and analytics

### Data Protection
- Industry-standard encryption for all transactions
- Secure payment processing through certified gateways
- Regular security audits and updates
- We never sell your personal information

## Terms of Service

### Order Acceptance
- Orders are subject to product availability
- We reserve the right to cancel orders for pricing errors or fraud
- All prices are in USD unless otherwise stated
- Taxes calculated based on shipping address

### Product Descriptions
- We strive for accuracy but cannot guarantee all product descriptions are error-free
- Product images are for illustration; actual items may vary
- Specifications subject to change without notice

### Limitation of Liability
- Our liability is limited to the purchase price of the item
- We are not responsible for indirect or consequential damages
- Product warranties are provided by manufacturers

## Payment Policy

### Accepted Methods
- Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Bank Transfer: Available for orders over $1000

### Payment Processing
- Charges occur when order is confirmed
- Pre-orders may be charged when item ships
- Failed payments result in order cancellation
- Refunds processed to original payment method

### Financing Options
- Installment plans available for orders over $500
- Subject to credit approval through financing partners
- Terms and interest rates vary by provider

## Customer Service

### Support Hours
- Email Support: 24/7 (response within 24 hours)
- Live Chat: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
- Phone Support: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST

- Level 2: Customer service representative
